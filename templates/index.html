Hackers and Painters?

hackersandpainters.sg is a lunch-time skills sharing event for UI/UX designers and software/hardware hackers to interact and share their skills and know-how in an informal setting. The emphasis is on skills/knowledge sharing over a short 30 minutes to 1 hour session and polished presentations are *not* expected.
